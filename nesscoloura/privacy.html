Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Jan, 2024

Thank you for choosing Nesscoloura, a mobile application dedicated to art enthusiasts. This Privacy Policy outlines how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use our application. By accessing and using Nesscoloura, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account on Nesscoloura, we collect your email address, password, profile picture, and bio. User accounts can be verified through a one-time password (OTP) sent to the provided email address.

1.2 Art-related Posts

Users have the option to upload art-related content such as pencil sketches and paintings. We do not allow the upload of images containing people.

1.3 Interaction Information

We collect data on user interactions within the app, including likes, comments, and shares on art-related posts.

1.4 Reporting and Blocking

Users can report accounts for inappropriate content or behavior. Blocking features are available to manage interactions with other users.

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Account Management

Your email address is used for account verification, communication, and account recovery purposes.

2.2 Art-related Content

The content you upload is used to facilitate sharing within the Nesscoloura community.

2.3 Interaction Features

Information about likes, comments, and shares is used to enhance user engagement and improve our services.

3. Sharing Your Information

3.1 Third-Party Services

We may share your information with third-party services to provide specific features (e.g., email verification).

3.2 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations, enforce our policies, or respond to legal requests.

4. Your Choices

4.1 Account Settings

You can manage and update your account information, privacy settings, and notification preferences directly within the app.

4.2 Deleting Your Account

If you wish to delete your Nesscoloura account, you can do so easily within the app settings. Deleting your account will permanently remove all your account data, including profile information and uploaded content.

5. Security

Nesscoloura employs industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices. Users will be notified of any significant updates.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact Yashas Gowda.

Thank you for being a part of the Nesscoloura community!
