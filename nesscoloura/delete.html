Delete Your Nesscoloura Account

We understand that you may occasionally need to delete your Nesscoloura account. Below are the steps to guide you through this process:

  1. Select the profile section located in the bottom navigation bar.
  2. Click on the additional options button located in the top right corner.
  3. Choose "Settings" from the menu.
  4. Navigate to the "Account" section within the settings screen.
  5. Click on the "Delete account" button.
  6. Select "Proceed" to permanently delete the account.

Please note that deleting your account is irreversible and will result in the permanent loss of your account data, including any associated content or preferences.

If you have any questions or encounter any difficulties during this process, feel free to contact our support team for assistance.
